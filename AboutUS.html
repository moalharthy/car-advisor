About Us


about Car Advisor


"Welcome to Sultan Qaboos University's Car Choice Assistant! We are a group of students committed to making your car-buying process as easy as possible.
Provide us with your driving preferences, location, and spending limit, and we will suggest the top three vehicles to you. We'll also search local dealerships for the greatest offers. moreover, our platform have a marketplace that you can Buy and Sell cars from, what special about our marketplace is we manually choose the cars. Therefore, the quality and authenticity of the market place is assured. Also, there is the "car news" section were we bring along all the car enthusiast community along. In this section we discuss and share the latest news about car and other related topics. Our service is simple, clear-cut, and hassle-free. Come with us as we look for your ideal vehicle."
